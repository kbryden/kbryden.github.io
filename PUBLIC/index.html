Interactive Kiosk

Role

Designer/
Project Manager

Agency

Shane Co.

Year

2016-2017

The interactive kiosk was installed January 2017. I started the project in July of 2016. During this process, I designed the interface and content. Put together the project proposal, management plan, sitemap, wireframes, and development approach. I handed off all designs and wireframes to the developers. Managed ordering the hardware and frames, putting in data drops, and installation. I worked with BOYD, Four Winds, Netsic, and our internal IT and Creative department.

Zone 5

Role

Designer/
Developer

Agency

MCAD

Year

2016

Zone 5 is a website I developed and designed. The purpose of the site is to provide a useful yet styled gardening guide for people who are living in zone 5a-5b. For this site, I created the strategy, site diagram, mood board, style guides, live wireframes, mockups, and the final site. The project was challenging and fun. I enjoyed mostly using my own photos and videos to fill the pages with content. I used HTML5, CSS, Javascript, and JQuery to complete the final site.
