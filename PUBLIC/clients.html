This website is in the making. I am working with a client to create a website that will allow for her services to be searchable via the web. She does not currently have a website so we will need to gather all of the necessary information to get started. Content can be difficult to obtain the first go round but I am confident that we will be able to create exactly what she needs.

Project Proposal:

Iron Horse Performance needs a website to provide easily accessible information to their customers. The website will help promote breeding, training, horse services such as Equi-resp and laser-treatment, mares and geldings for sale, and contact information. All pricing will be provided by contact only. The site will not post any prices.

Goals

The primary goal for the site is to promote Iron Horse Performance services and sales.

The secondary goal for the site is to provide useful information about the ranch that will help in the promotion and branding of Iron Horse Performance as a business.

Specifications

The site will have six main pages: breeding, training, experience, services, horses for sale, and contact. Each of these pages will feature high-res beautiful images, clean-inviting copy, and useful information pertaining to the main focus of the page.

  • Breeding

    The focus of this page will be on Renee’s prize stallion Black Jack Perk. We should focus on the horse’s pedigree and breeding information.

  • Training

    Barrel horse training will include information about how the horses are trained without giving away any trade secrets.

  • Experience

    There will be a page focused on Renee and her experience with horses.

  • Services

    Equi-resp will provide information regarding breathing exercises and the equipment used.

    Laser treatment services

  • Horses For Sale

    All assets under this page must be easy to remove/add.

    Page for mares will have full images of female horses and a small bio.

    Page for geldings (male castrated horses) will have full images of male horses and a small bio.

    Consignment?

  • Contact

    This page will provide basic contact information for customers to use.

Milestones

Gather all content and page information

Information and examples will be provided by Renee or Julie. Kristi will organize and design the information and present designs before starting development.

Development

I will develop the site based off client needs and approvals.

Final Assets

I will provide a video tutorial on how to add/remove content on the site.
