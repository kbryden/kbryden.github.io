Alerts

Creating Alert messages can be simple and easy. Each message appears when needed and should be capable of being closed once the message has been read. Alerts don't necessarily have to be warnings. They can also be positive reminders.

× Danger! Indicates a dangerous or potentially negative action.
× Warning! Indicates a warning or potentially negative action.
× Reminder! Indicates a reminder or neutral action.
