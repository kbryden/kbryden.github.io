Interactive Kiosk

Role

Designer/
Project Manager

Agency

Shane Co.

Year

2016-2017

The Interactive Kiosk was created to solve a major problem that retail stores were having especially with the advancement of technology. You wouldn’t think that technology would have much of an effect on a man’s decision to buy a diamond for his lady but it can be quite crippling to the process. The problem was that men were coming in and telling sales associates about a ring she wanted on social media. The solution was to present them with a kiosk so that they could browse and compare social media with Shane Co.’s online catalog. Now they can not only see all social posts but they can also see the company’s history and values. They are presented with not only a way to find what she really wants but also a reason to buy from Shane Co.

After observing the initial installation, I was surprised to find that customers were using the kiosk for many reasons. They used it when staff was busy. They used it out of pure curiosity. Customers were browsing our product and learning about our company instead of getting on their phone and getting distracted through social media and possibly even browsing competitors sites.
