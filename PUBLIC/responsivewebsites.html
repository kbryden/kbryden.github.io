Mobile first is a top priority in today’s website designs. More people access your website through their phone than through any other device. With new devices and screen sizes being developed and created everyday, companies can no longer afford to ignore this growing market. Especially with the increasing impatience from consumers who want it now.

These are responsive designs that have been fully developed by myself. Each design started with a mobile first initiative that grew to a full desktop version. Some of these sites were redesigns of poorly designed sites without any mobile compatibility. Other sites were researched and inspired by my own interests and needs of the internet. For these sites I created the wireframes, sitemaps, style guides, and the final site. For design, I used the Adobe Creative suite. For Development, I used Atom, HTML5, CSS, SASS, Javascript, JQuery, and Github to complete the final site.

Pete's Restaurants

This site was a redesign of a non-responsive site for a local restaurant chain. In redesigning this site, I tried to keep in mind the clientele and the vibe of their restaurants. The chain has a variety of foods not just greek. From their original site, you cannot tell that they have mostly american food and they have a Mexican restaurant as well. These restaurants are open 24/7 and have a dive vibe to them. Bar hoppers and late brunches are big fans of Pete’s restaurants. This is why the site is stripped down to the basics. The nav includes the location and menu for each location for an easy find.
